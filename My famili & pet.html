ੈ♡ My Family And Pet ੈ♡

In this particular pages, I am about to share insights into my family, beloved pet, and circle of friends.

To begin with, my father proudly identifies as a native of Pahang, an area that carries relevance in his life's story. My mother, on the other hand, is a native of the dynamic metropolis of Kuala Lumpur, and she brings a broad background to our family tapestry. In the fortunate year of 2000, the merging of these two dissimilar origins resulted in the pure tie of marriage. As they began their journey together, a celebration of love and devotion blossomed. The next chapter in our family's story witnessed the arrival of my elder brother in July of the following year. This joyous added a new depth to our family dynamic, strengthening the bonds that unite us. The unique basis upon which our familial tale continues to develop is the convergence of Pahang ancestry, the urban spirit of Kuala Lumpur, and the shared experiences of a growing family.


Family


DAD

My dad, who was born on July 19th, is the second child in his family's amazing lineup of ten siblings. What differentiates my dad is an unusual taste which is termites, known as "anai-anai" in Bahasa. Aside from that, my father has a unique talking style. His chatty personality turns every connection into a bright and exciting exchange, guaranteeing that conversations with him are always full of excitement and laughter.




MOM

My mother, who was born on February 19th, has an outstanding instance of hard work and multitasking ability. Her extraordinary capacity to handle many jobs at the same time indicates to her diligent personality. She takes her passion and devotion to community participation beyond the bounds of our house, enthusiastically participating in our residential community group. As I previously stated, her commitment to community service extends beyond simple participation which she served as Vice President of our residential community club. In that position, she made a substantial contribution to the well-being and unity of our neighbourhood. Not just that, my mum also has a chatty attitude that pours excitement into every conversation. Conversation with her is enjoyable since she easily weaves between topics with excitement. As a result, alongside as being a diligent and community-minded woman, my mother is very inspirational to me.




OLDER BROTHER

My brother, who was born on July 16th, plays an important position in our family as the person who always drives. His superb driving abilities and preparedness to take us to numerous places make him a significant help. Also can guaranteeing that we may easily and conveniently reach any location our hearts desire to. Comparing himself to our parents' outgoing personalities, my brother has a more limited communication style. While my mother and father thrive on energetic talks, my brother preferring to express himself openly when he feels like want to. This unique attitude adds a subtle dimension to our familial connections, which leads to a balanced range of communicating styles within our family.




YOUNGER BROTHER

My younger brother, who was born on February 20th, is the youngest member. He has the lovable reputation of being a bit privileged youngster. This treatment is given not only from our parents, but also from his loving sister and brother. We find satisfaction in pampering and babying him to shower him with attention. This common emotion points to the familial link that unites us, delivering a caring environment in which love and compassion flow freely. He gives a distinct warmth to our family as the youngest member.





Pet



LOLA

Lola, a male cat with the breed of British Shorthair (BSH), enter the world on May 5, 2023. His mother and father are both come from the same prestigious breed. Originally, Lola had three siblings, yet two of them died in December 2022 which Lisa and Koko. In terms of personality, Lola is fairly introverted. He keeps his meowing to a minimal, providing a calm and controlled presence in our family.




BELLA
Bella, who was born on December 16, 2022, is an attractive female cat that has a same breed as Lola, British Shorthair (BSH). Bella and Lola are related not just by their breed but also by their shared mother, who gave birth to Bella during her first pregnancy and Lola during her second. Tragically, the story takes a depressing turn as we reflect on Bella's unexpected death in December 2023. She suffered an untimely end when she was killed in a car crash. Bella's death put a cloud of sadness over the family, leaving behind memories of her brief but treasured presence. Despite the loss, her memory continues on.
